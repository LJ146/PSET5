Why do we care?

Disease and outbreaks have long been used to rationalize xenophobia. The pandemic is covid, but the pandemic is also racism. Just like an 89 years-old asian grandmother in Brooklyn who was burned by 2 young man, and recently the japanese pianist who was attacked on New York subway for being Chinese, anyone who is Asian or perceived to be Asian could be enough to provoke an attack.

Although all restaurants have been hit, Asian restaurants are the category that has been hit hardest not only by the pandemic, but also by racism.
